Burd: VR Game

This Unity game is made for Meta Quest 2 and contains three mini-games simulating the life of a bird.

Arm flapping motions is used in Race for forward locomotion, and the joystick controls for movement direction.
Feast and Plop uses the hand controllers to aim at targets.
